MY ACHIEVEMENT

My name is Kazeem Yusuf Olayiwola. I reside in Ibadan, Sanyo to be precise.Not to beat around the bush I just want to discuss about my achievement so far not that have achieved much but just want to let you know about the little i've achieved.

Firstly, I attended Arix Nursery and Primary school which is located at boluwaji.The school was not too fun for a start, meeting new faces was somehow strange but with time I got adapted to the environment and enjoyed their hospitality.I participated in school activities like cultural dance and playing football,when I got to primary five they made me the time-keeper and I graduated from Arix in the year 2010.

I got admission to senior secondary school it was so different and matured that was when I experienced different teachers teaching different subjects unlike primary school whereby you just have one teacher teaching every subjects.I also participated in so many activities in secondary school like going for debates and quiz competition.I was also part of the school team in my secondary school and we won a competition hosted by the private schools committee.My secondary school days was so fun I had many friends and experience unlike primary school.After two years graduating from senior secondary school, I got admission to kwara state university to study Library and Information Science(L.I.S). I joined the departmental team and played two competition but fourtunately for us we won one of the competition hosted by the head of department called HOD's cup hoping to win many trophies in the nearest future.May Allah grant me this opportunity.
